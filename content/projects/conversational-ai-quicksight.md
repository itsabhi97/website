### Overview
For a premier electrical contractor’s company, I transformed QuickBase data into Conversational AI Analytics using Amazon Q and Quicksight.

### Key Contributions
- Developed automated ETL processes using AWS Glue to extract data from Quickbase applications via API.
- Created a datalake in S3 with separate zones for raw and processed data.
- Implemented merge logic to maintain complete datasets while efficiently processing incremental updates.
- Leveraged Amazon QuickSight as a metadata enhancement layer for Amazon Q Business.
- Enhanced field definitions with business terminology, descriptions, and synonyms for natural language processing.
- Established an automated scheduling system to refresh data daily without manual intervention.
- Configured Amazon Q Business to deliver AI-powered insights, such as text summaries, visualizations, tables, and statistical analyses through a conversational interface.
