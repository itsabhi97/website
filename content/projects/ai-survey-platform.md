### Overview
For a company working in employee feedback and analytics, I built an AI Survey Summarisation Platform.

### Key Contributions
- Led the design and deployment of an AI survey summarisation platform using the AWS serverless stack (Lambda, Cognito, Bedrock).
- Integrated Claude Sonnet 4 via AWS Bedrock for high-accuracy, context-aware summarisation.
- Built context enrichment for multi-format docs (PDF, DOCX, XLSX) to enhance AI insights.
- Developed a data segmentation engine for customizable filtering and analysis.
- Delivered a self-service UI for uploads, filtering, summaries, and survey history.
- Created a natural language chat interface for real-time, contextual survey querying.
- Automated CI/CD with Terraform, CodePipeline, and CodeBuild.
- Drove cross-functional collaboration to align with business goals.
