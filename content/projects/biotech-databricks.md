### Overview
For a company working in the biotechnology domain, I maintained and optimized the Enterprise Data Platform in Databricks.

### Key Contributions
- Maintained and monitored existing data pipelines to ensure reliable and consistent data delivery.
- Managed stakeholder data generation processes, including configuring pipeline parameters and executing multiple dependent pipelines in sequence.
- Performed post-processing data validation to ensure accuracy, completeness, and removal of unnecessary records before delivery.
- Automated end-to-end pipeline orchestration, including sequential pipeline triggering and automated data validation upon completion.
- Reduced manual effort and overall data generation time by 50% by eliminating manual monitoring and intervention.
- Implemented an automated email notification system to alert users of pipeline completion, validation failures, or task-level errors, improving visibility and user experience for non-technical stakeholders.
